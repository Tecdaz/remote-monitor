# Wear OS app quality

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/docs/quality-guidelines/wear-app-quality
**Section**: Essentials → Quality

## What this page is

The **official quality checklist** for Wear OS apps — the requirements your app must meet to pass review on Google Play, plus recommended/best-practice items. Each requirement has a stable ID for team communication.

## Sections

- Requirements (the checklist — requirements you must meet)
- Frequently asked questions
- Version history (how the checklist has changed)

## When to consult

**Before submitting** a Wear OS app to Google Play, and as a self-audit before code-complete. Failure to meet requirements can lead to submission rejection.

## Source quote

> The checklists on this page show various requirements to help you assess the quality of your Wear OS app and follow the Principles of Wear OS development. Each requirement has a unique ID which you might find helpful to use when you communicate with your team. Failing to comply with all of the requirements might lead to rejection of your app submission.
