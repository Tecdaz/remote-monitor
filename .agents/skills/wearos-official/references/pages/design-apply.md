# Material 3 Expressive — Design language

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/design/ui/wear/guides/get-started/apply
**Section**: Design → Design language

## What this page is

Deeper guidance on the **Material 3 Expressive design language** for Wear — how to actually apply color, typography, shape, and motion in a wearable UI. The "why" behind the tokens.

## Sections

- Color and typography (deeper tonal palettes, variable font axes)
- Shape and motion (expressive shape system, animated transitions)

## When to consult

When choosing color tokens, typography scale, or shapes for a screen. Useful for design reviews to justify visual decisions against the spec.

## Source quote

> Material 3 Expressive (M3) was built to meet user demand for experiences that are modern, relevant, and distinct. Expressive also allows designers to mirror specific emotions and feelings in the layout and presentation of the interface.
