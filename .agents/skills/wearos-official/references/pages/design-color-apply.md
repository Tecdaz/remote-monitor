# Apply color — Pair and layer

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/design/ui/wear/guides/styles/color/apply
**Section**: Design → Styles → Color → Apply

## What this page is

How to **apply Material 3 Expressive color tokens** correctly — pairing primary/secondary/tertiary accents with neutral surface colors, maintaining visual hierarchy through hue/chroma/tone, and keeping adequate contrast.

## Sections

- Pair and layer colors (primary, secondary, tertiary accents vs surface neutrals)

## When to consult

When defining a theme, choosing color tokens, or auditing contrast. Critical rule: only use colors in their intended pairs — combining colors improperly may break contrast.

## Source quote

> To maintain visual accessibility, apply colors only in the intended pairs color tokens. Combining colors improperly may break contrast necessary for visual accessibility, particularly when colors are adjusted through dynamic color.
