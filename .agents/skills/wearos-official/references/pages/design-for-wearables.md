# Design for wearables (overview)

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/design/ui/wear/guides/get-started/design-for-wearables
**Section**: Design → Get started

## What this page is

A high-level **overview of designing for the watch form factor** — use cases, design-for-the-round-screen guidance, and platform capabilities/limitations.

## Sections

- Understand use cases (activity goals, instant messages, quick info)
- Design for the round screen (specific challenges of circular displays)
- Resources

## When to consult

At the very start of a Wear design project — read this before any pixel work. The "Design for the round screen" section is critical if you've never designed for a circular display.

## Source quote

> As a wearable platform, Wear OS helps people live more present, healthy, and productive lives. Wear OS helps users stay connected online and complete tasks quickly, while leaving their phones in their pockets.
