## What this page is

The **top-level hub for Wear OS design** — entry point to Material 3 Expressive, surface design (apps + tiles), common layouts, styles, and Figma kits. Treat this as the table of contents for design work.

## When to consult

When starting design work — the hub links to every guide. Read the hero ("Watches enable users to get information at a glance") to anchor the mission.
