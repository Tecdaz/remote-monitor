# Levels of expression

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/design/ui/wear/guides/get-started/levels-expression
**Section**: Design → Get started → Material 3 Expressive

## What this page is

Defines the **three tiers of expression** in Material 3 Expressive: **foundational**, **excellent**, and **transformational**. Use these tiers as a self-rating for how expressive your app's UI is — target excellent or transformational.

## Sections

- Foundational (meets the spec)
- Excellent (the target for shipping apps)
- Transformational (signature / best-in-class)

## When to consult

In design reviews, as a calibration tool. "What tier is this screen hitting?" is a useful question to keep the bar high without bikeshedding.

## Source quote

> Material 3 Expressive introduces three tiers of expression: foundational, excellent and transformational. Use these tiers to gauge the level of expression being achieved. Target the excellent or transformative level for your app to achieve optimal expression.
