# Tiles

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/tiles
**Section**: Surfaces → Tiles

## What this page is

The official developer guide for **Tiles** — the swipeable, non-scrollable cards next to the watch face that give quick access to actions and at-a-glance info. Entry point for `TileService`, layout, and the Material 3 Expressive tile components.

## Sections

- Core principles (non-scrollable, glanceable, single tap action)
- Recommended for you (related links)

## When to consult

When planning a Tile for quick actions (timer, workout start, weather). Critical constraint to remember: **tiles cannot scroll** — content must fit.

## Source quote

> Tiles provide quick access to the information and actions users need to get things done. The tiles carousel is revealed by a swipe on the watch face, and additional swipes will switch between tiles. Tiles themselves cannot be scrolled.
