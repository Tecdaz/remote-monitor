# Design experiences for kids on Wear OS

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/design/ui/wear/guides/m2-5/foundations/wear-os-for-kids
**Section**: Design → Kids

## What this page is

Specialized design principles for apps/watch faces **targeting kids** on Wear OS — overlay on top of the standard Wear principles to handle engagement, safety, and content appropriateness.

## Sections

- Design principles for kids
- Content principles for kids
- Appealing
- Enriching

## When to consult

Only when the target audience is children. Most Wear apps can ignore this.

## Source quote

> Building apps and watch faces for kids requires a careful consideration of both the principles of Wear OS development, and the principles set forth on this page.
