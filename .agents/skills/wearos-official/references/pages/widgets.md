# Wear Widgets

**URL**: https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/widgets
**Section**: Surfaces → Widgets

## What this page is

The official landing for **Wear Widgets** — the new (Wear OS 7-era) widget surface powered by **Jetpack Glance** and the **Remote Compose framework**. Two card sizes: small (2×1) and large (2×2). Designed to align with mobile widgets for cross-device consistency.

## Sections

- Next steps (links to get_started + migration guides)

## When to consult

When adding a widget to a Wear app. The new (preferred) approach is Glance-based widgets, not the legacy Tiles ProtoLayout — see `widgets-migration.md` for moving from tiles to widgets.

## Source quote

> Full-screen tiles have been a go-to surface on Wear OS, providing users with instant, glanceable access to their essential updates. As the Android ecosystem moves toward a unified vision for widgets, we're bringing the watch closer to the rest of the Android family.
